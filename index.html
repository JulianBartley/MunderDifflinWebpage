Who we are

Our founders, Robert Dunder and Robert Mifflin, in 1949 once told each other, "Anyone can sell paper!" This is why we look past office products and focus on the customer's experience...because anyone can sell paper. We inspire innovaters and forward thinkers.

TEAM GREEN

We have dedicated ourselves to putting back everything we take from the planet. We have planted trees in each country and will continue. Our main focus is currently in the Amazon and planting more greenery in major cities.
